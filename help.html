How do I start taking surveys?
Log in to your account and go to the Surveys page. Click "Take Survey" on any available survey and follow the instructions. Earnings are added automatically upon completion.
What are the requirements to join SurveyEarn?
Anyone aged 18+ with a valid email address can join. Registration is free and takes less than 2 minutes.
How much can I earn per survey?
Survey rewards vary by type. System surveys (free) offer Ksh 170–199, while premium surveys can earn you Ksh 350–760 per survey. Higher rewards are available for longer surveys.
When and how can I withdraw my earnings?
Withdrawals are available once you reach a minimum of Ksh 500. You can withdraw via M-Pesa, PayPal, or bank transfer. Payments are processed within 24-48 hours.
Why do I have to wait 7 seconds between questions?
This is a quality measure to prevent random clicking and ensure thoughtful responses. It helps maintain high data quality for our clients.
What happens if I lose internet connection during a survey?
If you reconnect within a few minutes, you may be able to resume. Otherwise, the survey may be lost. We recommend a stable connection before starting.
How do I update my profile information?
Go to Settings and click on Profile Information. You can edit your name, email, and other details. Keep your profile complete to receive relevant surveys.
Can I have multiple accounts?
No, each user is allowed only one account. Duplicate accounts may result in suspension.
How does the referral program work?
Share your unique referral code. When a friend signs up and completes their first survey, you both earn Ksh 200.
